- The commit-subject extractor (`extract_commit_subject`) no longer mis-reads the subject for three standard `git commit` shapes: a single `-m` with embedded newlines (now takes the first line, not the whole subject+body blob), multiple `-m` flags (now the first value, not the greedy last = body), and a commit chained after an unrelated `cat <<EOF` heredoc (the sibling heredoc is no longer grabbed as the subject). Extraction is bounded to the `git commit` segment and runs on the raw command; `-F`-only commits extract empty and fail open. SPEC §6.1.1 documents the contract. (#367)
